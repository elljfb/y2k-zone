What Was the Millennium Bug?<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Caused by Early Computer Systems Using Two-Digit Dates<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

At the heart of the Y2K bug lay a fundamental flaw rooted in the early computer systems. Many systems, to conserve memory, represented years with only two digits. As the calendar approached the year 2000, these systems faced a critical challenge, unable to distinguish between the turn of the century and a return to 1900.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
